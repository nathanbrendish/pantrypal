# ShelfLife — Supabase Auth email templates

Update these in the Supabase Dashboard → Authentication → Email Templates.

Site URL: `https://myshelflife.co.uk`

## Confirm signup

**Subject:** Verify your ShelfLife account

**Body (excerpt):** Welcome to ShelfLife. Confirm your email to get started.

## Magic link / Invite

**Subject:** Your ShelfLife sign-in link

## Reset password

**Subject:** Reset your ShelfLife password

**Body (excerpt):** Reset your ShelfLife password using the link below.

## Change email

**Subject:** Confirm your new ShelfLife email
